Roadmap to become a Security Engineer

1st step:
MAKE YOUR MIND
It's important to make up your mind to become a security professional. This profession is not for everyone, and thus not everyone is eligible for it. One should keep this in mind: all the depictions in the movies About hacking are 95% edited, and the most important aspect of hacking, patience and hard work, is always omitted.
2nd step:
MARGIN OF REEL AND REAL
There are many myths that the media spreads about ethical hacking: that we are the ones who get money on the phone line and scam people, or that we can hack anyones identity with a click of a button from our PC or we are the ones who sit in a dark room with a flash of black screen and some green fonts. There are some myths that need to be revealed about the person pursuing this as a profession.
3rd step:
KNOW YOUR LEVEL
It's quite important to understand at what point you can understand things better than others. Any student reading this might have concerns related to configuring his or her level. The thing is how efficiently When you solve a problem, you rise above all others.
4rth step:
START QUESTIONING
Definitely, a question or doubt is enough to make an object or thing vulnerable if it's based on beliefs but not on facts. Start questioning with the intention of getting an answer, no matter how far you go for it. Search your queries and know the answers. Keep your basics strong and remember that a person with no doubts will never acknowledge anything.
5th step:(biased situation based )
IM A 10th CLASS STUDENT CAN I DO HACKING?
The reader may have this type of question, like, If I'm an 8th grader or from a medical field, can I become a hacker? Listen carefully: anyone with intel can become a hacker. and theres a warning that one should not "fantasise" the world of hacking. Be determined. It's nothing like you chose this line to be sure that it interests you or not. yes you can become a hacker even if you are a 10th grader . but you should know the fundamentals of hacking
6th step:
PRE-REQUISITE TO BECOME A HACKER
In order to become a skilled hacker, one must have a good knowledge of how networks actually work, some programming languages to create scripts, different operating systems,Hardware skills etc.

STARTING WITH...

Things to remember
Being an ethical hacker or a security engineer is not so easy; you have to read tonnes of books and gain lots of experience in order to climb the ladder of competition. Remember that patience is essential; if you lose it, you must start over.
Understanding hardware
In order to learn hacking, the learner must know the concepts of how hardware works. What are the main characteristics of a motherboard, how does a GPU work, what is a registry, and how is it linked to hardware? In order to learn more about it, certifications like CompTIA A+ can be handy.
Ways to communicate with computer
A hacker must know how to communicate with the computer, and in order to do so, he must be familiar with one of the computer programming languages, like Python, Java,java C++, etc.; less the person knows that he can create scripts and his own tools depending on how deeply he understands any programming language.
Computer networks
Isn't it a mind-boggling instance that we all use WhatsApp and Instagram but we don't know how a message sent from one device reaches another? In order to hack a network, one should learn the procedures of computer networks. To learn the same certifications like compTIA NETWORK+,CCNA and CCNP is recommended.
Hacker's paradice:KALI
After learning about the hardware, it's time to work with the OS, and the majority of a hacker's focus should be on Windows and Kali Linux. What is Kali Linux? its and the operating system's design to provide vulnerability analysis on any software, website, application, etc.
Earn your first certification
After months of understanding the basics of hacking, understanding terminologies and definitions, and answering the questions available online, it's time to grab your first certification that will act as your entry into the world of hacking. Certified ethical hacker, or C|EH, is the certification provided by the EC Council that ensures that a person is now eligible to enter the world of ethical hacking.
Level up
Once you're done with C|EH, try hackathons for the bigger level, and then move on to certifications like CHFI, CompTIA Security+, etc. and start applying for bug bounty programmes.
