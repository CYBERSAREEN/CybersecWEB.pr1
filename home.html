What is Ethical Hacking?
Ans: Ethical hacking refers to the process of using hacking techniques and tools to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in computer systems and networks. Unlike malicious hacking, ethical hacking is done with the permission and knowledge of the system or network owner in order to improve the security of the system.
Why do we need Cyber Security?
Ans: Learning cyber security is essential in today's digital age because it helps us protect our personal and professional information from cyber threats. As we become more reliant on technology, the risk of cyber attacks also increases. Cyber security education equips individuals with the knowledge and skills to prevent, detect, and respond to cyber attacks, safeguarding their sensitive data and assets.
